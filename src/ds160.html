DS-160 Visa Application Form

Application for Nonimmigrant Visa

This is the visa application required to be filled out prior to an interview at the embassy or consulate.
Filling out this form does not imply one will be granted a visa.



Before submitting the application, please review your entries below.
Once you have reviewed all sections, click Submit Application to complete the application process.

If you have reached this point successfully, you would have completed 2 out of 8 sections required for this form.

The sections that follow include questions regarding your work experience, family, education, languages you speak, non-profits you support, mental and physical health, etc.

In 1994, the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA) -today, United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement (USMCA)- was proclaimed.

This agreement intended to set the ground for these 3 countries to collaborate and grow together.

As a byproduct, TN visas were created to facilitate mobility for American, Canadian, and Mexican professionals in this region.
