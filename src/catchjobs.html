Since 2020, hundreds of thousands of layoffs have happened across the tech industry.

A field that was once deemed as prolific is now collapsing due to events such as the rise of AI, lower consumer spending, overhiring during the pandemic and higher interest rates.

Many people who once thought tech was a safe place, now find themselves looking for jobs for longer periods in smaller pools of reqs.

For those of us on a work visa, each day without a job during a 60-day grace period is a day closer to a big chapter of our lives ending soon.
